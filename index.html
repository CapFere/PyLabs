What is "Py Labs?"

The concept behind the Py Labs is to energize, encourage and empower students, in their current state with the power to code. The power-to-create is strong within every young person and many just love this opportunity to begin creating professional products through code. So why do we have to wait until the education system becomes perfect before we begin creating opportunity, opening minds to limitless possibilities and teaching new and exciting courses to the students?
